THE GYM

I no longer play any sports competively. However, I workout at the gym between 4/5 times a week. I am a member of both the Maradyke, Cork and Fehily's Fitness, Ballincollig.


Training at the gym can be a solitary experience and I do miss the comraradre of team sports, but I feel maintaining a healthy body through regular excercise is critical in maintaining a healthy mind and lifestyle.


I don't work out for any competition nor do I work out for any clear goal. I enjoy the gym because it keeps me focused, energetic and ultimately - it keeps me happy.
